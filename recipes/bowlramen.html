Making the best bowl of Bowl Ramen in your life!

A very simple change makes all the difference in the world

Ingredients

  • Bowl Ramen (I recommend the Hot & Spicy)
  • Boiling, hot water
  • Chopsticks (don't eat with a fork)

Instructions

  1. Open the pack of bowl ramen
  2. Open the sauce packets inside and spread around the ramen noodles
  3. Fill the bowl with water but don't fill it to the line; fill it 1 centimeter below the line
  4. Close the lid with chopsticks. If you use anything else, that's considered death in Asia
  5. Open 5 minutes later so the noodles soaked up all the broth
  6. Enjoy!
